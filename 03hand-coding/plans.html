Plans

We selected a modified version of the Washington Farmhouse plan from Ivory Homes for our house. Our main modification was a massive expansion of the garage to accommodate Ryan's woodshop. Pushing the dimensions of the shop also allowed us to expand nearly all of the rooms in the house by two feet. It feels a bit silly to be building such a large house for just the two of us now that we're empty nesters, but we also want this to be our forever home and the place our kids return to for summer hiking adventures and winter ski vacations.
