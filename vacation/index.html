Welcome to Adventure Trails Travel Agency

Explore the World's Most Breathtaking Hiking Destinations

At Adventure Trails, we believe that the best way to experience the world's natural beauty is on foot. Since our founding in 1995, we've been dedicated to crafting unforgettable hiking adventures that immerse our clients in the stunning landscapes of Utah, Mont Blanc, New Zealand, Iceland, Norway, and the Dolomites. Whether you're a seasoned hiker or a nature lover seeking a new adventure, we have the perfect trail waiting for you.

About Us

Adventure Trails was born from a passion for exploring the great outdoors and a desire to share that love with others. Our founders, avid hikers themselves, envisioned a travel agency that not only guided people through magnificent landscapes but also fostered a deep appreciation for nature. Over the past 30 years, we've grown from a small local business into a trusted name in adventure travel, known for our personalized service, expert guides, and carefully curated itineraries.

Popular Destinations

  1. Utah: Discover the dramatic red rock formations, vast canyons, and unique desert landscapes of Zion, Bryce Canyon, and Arches National Parks.
  2. Mont Blanc: Traverse the highest peaks of the Alps, enjoying breathtaking views and charming alpine villages along the way.
  3. New Zealand: Experience the diverse landscapes from lush forests and rugged coastlines to majestic mountains and serene lakes.
  4. Iceland: Explore the land of fire and ice with its volcanic landscapes, geothermal springs, and cascading waterfalls.
  5. Norway: Hike through the stunning fjords, pristine forests, and dramatic cliffs of this Scandinavian gem.
  6. Dolomites: Marvel at the jagged peaks, serene alpine meadows, and picturesque villages in the heart of the Italian Alps.
